About

A Landmark Recognition

The Hyderabad Startup Ecosystem Awards 2026 brings together the city's founders, investors, policymakers and institutions for a single evening of recognition — held at Raj Bhavan, under the patronage of His Excellency the Honourable Governor of Telangana, Shri. Shiv Pratap Shukla.

Sixteen honours span deep tech, fintech, healthtech, consumer platforms, social impact and enterprise SaaS, alongside special recognitions for individuals and institutions who have shaped Hyderabad's standing as a national centre of enterprise.

The evening also marks the launch of "The CEO She Never Knew She Was" by Dr. Uday Murthy Bhogadi, unveiled by His Excellency the Honourable Governor of Telangana, Shri. Shiv Pratap Shukla.
